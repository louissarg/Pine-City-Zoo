KOALA

Koalas are marsupials native to Australia, known for their distinctive appearance and behavior. They have a stout, tailless body, large head with round, fluffy ears, and a large black nose. Their fur is thick and soft, typically gray or brown in color.

Koalas are primarily herbivores, feeding almost exclusively on eucalyptus leaves. They have a slow metabolism and spend most of their time sleeping or resting in trees to conserve energy. Koalas are known for their unique vocalizations, which can be quite loud and are used to communicate with each other.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?
